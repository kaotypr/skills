## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does PaySync compare to Aspire or Airwallex?**
PaySync is built specifically for Singapore SMEs expanding into Southeast Asia. While Aspire and Airwallex serve a broad global market, our product, pricing, and support are optimised for the regional corridors and compliance requirements that matter most to your business.

**Is my money safe with PaySync?**
Yes. PaySync is licensed by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) as a Major Payment Institution. Customer funds are safeguarded in accordance with MAS regulations.

**Can I switch plans at any time?**
Absolutely. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. No lock-in contracts.

**What currencies are supported?**
The Free plan supports 1 currency (SGD). Growth supports 5 currencies across key SEA markets. Enterprise plans can access our full range of supported currencies. Contact us for the latest list.

**Do corporate cards come with the Growth plan?**
Yes. The Growth plan includes corporate cards that you can issue to team members, with spend controls and real-time tracking.

**What happens if I exceed 50 transactions on the Free plan?**
You will be prompted to upgrade to the Growth plan. We will never block a pending transaction without notice.
