Running an online shop on Facebook and Instagram means your phone never stops buzzing. Customer messages, order confirmations, stock checks — it adds up fast.

Every time a customer places an order through your page, ShopFlow logs it automatically — with the customer's name, items, amount, and status. No more screenshots. No more manual lists.

Import your product list so ShopFlow can track your inventory in real time. When stock runs low, you'll get a heads-up before you have to tell a customer "sorry, sold out na."

Create templates for your most common replies — order received, payment confirmed, item shipped. ShopFlow sends them for you, so your customers feel taken care of even when you're busy.
