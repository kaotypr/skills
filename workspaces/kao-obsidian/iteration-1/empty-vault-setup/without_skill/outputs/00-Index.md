## Folders

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **00-Inbox** | Capture quick thoughts, unsorted notes. Process regularly. |
| **01-Projects** | Active projects with a clear goal and deadline. |
| **02-Areas** | Ongoing areas of responsibility (e.g., team leadership, architecture). |
| **03-Resources** | Reference material, how-tos, snippets, and learning notes. |
| **04-Archive** | Completed or inactive projects and notes. |
| **Templates** | Note templates for daily notes, meetings, etc. |
| **Daily Notes** | Auto-generated daily journal entries. |
| **Meetings** | Meeting notes, organized by date. |

## Tips

- Use `Ctrl/Cmd + O` to quickly open any note.
- Use `Ctrl/Cmd + P` to open the command palette.
- Press `Alt + Click` on a `[[link]]` to open it in a new pane.
- Process your **00-Inbox** folder weekly — move or link notes to the right place.
