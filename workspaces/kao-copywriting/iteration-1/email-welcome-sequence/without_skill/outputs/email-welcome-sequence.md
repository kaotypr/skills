## Email 1: Welcome & Quick Win (Sent immediately after signup)

**Subject:** Welcome to FlowBoard — here's how to run your first async standup in 5 minutes

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

Welcome to FlowBoard! You just took the first step toward fewer meetings and more focused work for your team.

We built FlowBoard because we believe remote teams shouldn't need a 30-minute call just to answer "what are you working on today?"

Here's how to get your first async standup running in the next 5 minutes:

1. **Create your team workspace** — Click "New Workspace" from your dashboard and invite your team members by email.
2. **Set up your first standup** — Choose a check-in time (we recommend 9:00 AM in each person's local timezone) and pick your questions. Most teams start with three: What did you finish yesterday? What are you working on today? Anything blocking you?
3. **Let FlowBoard handle the rest** — Your team gets a simple prompt at the time you choose. Responses are collected and shared in one clean summary. No calendar invite required.

That's it. Tomorrow morning, you'll have a clear picture of what everyone is working on — without pulling anyone into a call.
